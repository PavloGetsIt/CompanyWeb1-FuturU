It's self-hypnosis at its core.

By repeatedly listening and or watching memories from your past or with new media, you slowly begin to ingrain a new mindset and believe system. The images, words and music subconsiously and unconsiously convince into a new way of thinking. Inturn, the pain threshold for taking action is lowered, and taking action towards goals that previously caused immense anxiety, are now more managable.

How will this help me?

In the 21st century people grow and mature amidst an environment and world that is not very conducive to accomplishing personal goals that require devote attention. Whether the goals are involved with a persons health, relationships, mindset or money, in a distracted world, it is a long slow rollercoaster of a battle. But we can change this by creating our own environments that force us into mindsets that prevent us from being distracted and rather already convinced that we will accomplish our goals.

This is crazy?! How could this work?

It works the same way everyone has acquired their current mindset and belives; through their environment. Within 1-3 months of listening or watching a minimum of 2 times daily you will begin to act, associate with and live in a way that is tailored to your goals. It requires as much willpower as pressing a single button and I certainly believe have enough energy to do that. Throughout the process, you will find out a lot about your life and what is bringing you up and down.

What option is better, Affirmation or Mindmovie?

Mind movies do a much better job of spiking your emotions because you're able to replay the scenes in your mind as yourself. Think superhero movies. Now you're the hero. But affirmations are just as effective if you're the type to just leave the audio playing in the background. Your mind doesn't need direct focus towards either, but it certainly helps you memorize each affirmation if you set a time during the day to sit and listen or watch.
